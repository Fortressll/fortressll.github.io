American Accent Training

# Sep 14, 2019

  • 最近在看一个微课，关于英语口语的，都是从《老友记》、《生活大爆炸》之类各种各样美剧中摘的句子，内容都是一些美国人生活中很常用的表达，觉得很受用所以在这里记录一下~

  • 作为一个程序猿，无论是看论文还是日常，英语真的是超级重要，如果能讲一口American accent将在很大程度上给自身起到一个加分的作用，在这里推荐一本书，国内有引进，感兴趣可以买来翻着看看~

如果不想看书，也可以去Youtube或者B站搜一个叫Coach Shane的大叔，讲的超级棒

  • 废话不多说，下面开始了！

Lesson1

  1. –When’s it due? –It’s due tomorrow.
  2. I was supposed to keep a journal all summer. It’s due today.
  3. What time is the next bus due?
  4. The next meeting is due to be held in three month’s time.
  5. What time is the Minister Lee due to arrive?
  6. And out rent is due…Yesterday.
  7. –When’s your due date? –Not until the early fall.

Lesson2

  1. Tell me a bit about yourself.
  2. –Would you like cream in your coffee? –Yes,please,just a bit.
  3. I’m just going out for a bit. See you later.
  4. I thought I could tell you a little about myself.
  5. Magazines are my passions, ever since I was a kid.
  6. I know most of your magazines inside out.
  7. All I really want is a chance or a position in your company.
  8. –Where do you see yourself in 10 years? –I want to be the best and work for a company where I can build a career.
  9. –Do you have any questions for us? –None that I can think of.
